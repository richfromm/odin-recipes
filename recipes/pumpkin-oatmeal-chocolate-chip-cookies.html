Pumpkin Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Adapted from https://www.mybakingaddiction.com/pumpkin-oatmeal-cookies/

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter; softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup pure pumpkin puree
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ginger
  • ½ teaspoon allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon cloves
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 12 oz. dark chocolate chips (1 typical package)
  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheets with a silicone mat or parchment paper.
  2. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar in a large mixer bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Add pumpkin, egg, and vanilla extract; mix well.
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, spices, and salt in a medium bowl.
  5. Add dry ingredients; combine until all ingredients are incorporated.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips and oats.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoons, or use a cookie scoop, onto prepared baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 16-18 minutes or until cookies are lightly browned. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
